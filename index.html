AT OUR SURF SCHOOL YOU WILL RECEIVE

TRAINING AND EQUIPMENT

All equipment and supplies are from well-known manufacturers and are provided for the entire course of the program.. The training process begins with determining your skiing level

ACCOMMODATION

Our surf school is located 100 m from the ocean coast. It features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. The upper terrace offers ocean views. Distance to city center 15 min.

FLIGHT AND INSURANCE

All program participants are insured by fins.kz insurance company. Flights from Tenerife to Lanzarote and back are included in the price of the program

CONQUER THE PEAKS

Our coaches are a team of experienced and certified professionals who live surfing and are ready to share their knowledge. Each of them has years of experience riding waves and working with students of all levels. They will not only help you master surfing techniques, but will also teach you to correctly understand the ocean, read the waves and feel confident on the board.
